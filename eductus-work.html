Youth Leadership Community

The Youth Leadership Community is a hub of social projects and a community of young people that supports the development of social initiatives and youth empowerment through conferences, challenges and interactive events. CPJ has already reached young people from all over Brazil through its events, and the Social Projects Hub is being developed as a proposal for 2022.

Mentoriza

Mentoriza is an initiative created with the purpose of offering personal and academic support, as well as vocational guidance to low-income students, with the aim of preparing them for admission to higher education and for the next stages of their lives. Currently, Mentoriza has a cycle of academic mentorships and a cycle of vocational guidance. The project has national reach and has already reached participants from all states of Brazil.

Eductus Lab

Currently, our project in development stage is Eductus Lab, which proposes to be a digital platform with offer for public and private schools, so that students have access to Learning Paths related to the general competences of the BNCC and the skills needed by 21st century students.
