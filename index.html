Eductus is a social impact organization that aims to support low-income Brazilian students on the development of their human, academic and professional potential.

Our vision

One day, all Brazilian students will have access to an education focused on forming young people with the skills necessary for their human, academic and professional development.

Our Work

Eductus operates on a national scale by offering its services and projects through the use of digital technologies. Currently, we work with 3 initiatives:

Mentoriza

Mentoriza is an initiative created with the purpose of offering personal and academic support, as well as vocational guidance to low-income students, with the aim of preparing them for admission to higher education and for the next stages of their lives.

Youth Leadership Community

The Youth Leadership Community is a hub of social projects and a community of young people that supports the development of social initiatives and youth empowerment through conferences, challenges and interactive events.

Eductus Lab

Eductus Lab is being developed as a digital platform for public and private schools to help students learn about 21st century skills.
